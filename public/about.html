About Us

Our Vision

To see a people transformed by the power of the word of God.

Our Mission

To share biblical insights to spur people to faith, action, and perseverance for transformation of lives and societies.

Our Story

Living Water Ministries was born out of a divine calling - a vision received from the Lord to create a space where believers and seekers alike could find spiritual nourishment and growth.

Through this blog, we aim to share biblical insights, personal testimonies, and practical teachings that will strengthen your faith, deepen your prayer life, and inspire you to share the gospel with others.

We believe that God's Word is living water that refreshes the soul, transforms the mind, and empowers believers to live victoriously. Our content is designed to help you:

  • Develop a deeper relationship with God through faith and prayer
  • Understand and apply biblical principles to everyday life
  • Grow in spiritual maturity and character
  • Share your faith effectively and win souls for Christ
  • Find encouragement and hope in challenging times
